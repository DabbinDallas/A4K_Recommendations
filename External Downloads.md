> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eb62331f-a9c9-436d-a605-d31148b7e8d2" />
> 3. Open Movies Directory and/or TV Shows Directory
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bddd0087-14a5-4796-b5db-51806eeaf1c5" />

> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eaf35a51-bdbc-4ec6-bb80-ab65cc5606db" />

> [!NOTE]
> ## Download Directions:
> 1. Navigate to the movie or episode of the show youd like to download (when you choose an episode for download you'll have the choose to download the whole season pack)
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a35d634b-23e0-4e0f-8b3c-78d41aa45be4" />
> 2. Hover over your selection and Press and hold your remotes select button (TV) or Right click (PC) to open the context menu
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8941d8f3-dc2a-4e21-a246-2a12c58da458" />
> 3. In the context menu click on options
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0aad2e76-efdc-4ba1-9924-535ac9b7a772" />
> 4. then click rescrape & select source
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e8d344a3-13d4-4229-b695-cc1af6bd6138" />
> 5. Choose the file you'd like to download and Repeat Step 2
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/464cbbc2-34af-4df8-86f0-9eaa68e8e55b" />
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/54990e6b-4af5-47f8-875f-afa3db216eda" />
> 6. in this menu click on download file (or download pack if youd like to download the whole season of a show)
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1b28db40-e034-4919-8933-a43a8de0738b" />
> 7. select continue with download
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4e3926df-e51a-459f-ad36-cb76bc1e18d7" />
> 8. if youd like you can rename the title of the folder on the page that comes up and then press OK when done
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0781ad33-7d12-40e9-9b51-b12cf5407297" />
> 9. After youve started the download you'll occasionally see popups in the top right that show your progress and a final popup telling you when the download is finished
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/77d0a7bd-2df5-43be-b204-cb9830da966e" />
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/30613040-d39f-4d0c-bc07-74e3eb9ed4c6" />
> 10. To find your download in POV, go back to the main menu of POV and click on Downloads
