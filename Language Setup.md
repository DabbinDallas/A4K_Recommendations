# How to get video files to play as English everytime ###
#
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Follow the steps below
> 1. On the homepage of Kodi go to the Settings (⚙️)
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/88d15f16-7469-4119-bf38-33ea8e9339e1" />
> 2. Click on Player
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/49d15e36-440e-476f-8be9-f6e01090fbcc" />
> 3. On the Left side scroll/click on Language
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fbc1fc14-3700-4372-b1e2-125c05765178" />
> 4. In the Audio section, Click on "Preferred audio language"
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/44a29bec-05e9-4802-a2c7-46d2eb1945b5" />
> 5. Either choose "User interface language" OR if your still having problems then come back into the menu and scroll down and click on "English"
> <img width="2560" height="1440" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/555d5684-c98e-4449-9204-afa481c09fe6" />
